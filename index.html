What are self driving cars?

Self driving cars are vehicles that are capable of driving without any human influence or input.
Self driving cars have Sensor that check their environment. If there are any obstacles around them, they try to find a way not to hit them.
Most self driving cars today are electric cars as well. But wether the vehicle is electric or not, the technique works mostly the same. Electric cars are just more modern than cars with gasoline.
But there are also hybrid cars which use both - gasoline and electricity.
