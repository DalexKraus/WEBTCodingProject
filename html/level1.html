The first level

At this first level, the car can help the driver with some functions
but the driver still handles accelerating, braking, and monitoring of the sourrounded area.
Think of a vehicle that brakes a little bit when you get to close to another car on the highway.
Most of the new cars are at least level 1. Manufactureres like BMW, VW and Audi don't even sell any Cars that are not at least level 1.

How often is it used?

How often is it used? Actually, Driver Assistance can be found in nearly every modern car of the well-known brands BMW, VW, Audi etc. Just think about the camera that helps you to fit into the parking slot: This is Level 1!
